# Who Are We

**XEONpvp** is a team of passionate Minecraft PvP enthusiasts dedicated to providing an exciting, fair, and competitive gaming environment. Our goal is to create a community where players of all skill levels can enjoy fast-paced, action-packed PvP matches without the distractions of pay-to-win mechanics. 

Our team is committed to providing regular updates, a variety of gameplay modes, and an overall clean and enjoyable PvP experience for both **Java** and **Bedrock** Edition players.
